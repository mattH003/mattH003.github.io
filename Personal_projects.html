Advanced Manufacturing Training program

Completed a 7-month machining training program while working full-time at Parker Aerospace, gaining approximately 200 hours of hands-on experience with manual mills (Prototrak), engine lathes, and Haas CNC mills and lathes. The program also included online coursework covering quality control, part inspection, and GD&T fundamentals. Fully funded by GE Aerospace and MassHire, the program concluded with NIMS certification exams validating the skills acquired throughout the training.
