Northeastern Aerospace Club (Redshift Propulsion Team 2024)

The Redshift Propulsion team as of fall 2024 set out to build and design a new LOX/KERO ablative engine to be used in a rocket for the foot per dollar challenge. My duties for this term are to design, manufacture and test the engine's injector with two of my peers. We first set off to make a swirl injector but have recently decided to instead go for an impinging injector. The CAD model above represents our SLA printed version of the injector that will be used for waterflow testing.
