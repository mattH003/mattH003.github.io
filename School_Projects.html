Statics Bridge

Final project for my statics class. I was tasked to improve upon a standard bridge designs(Truss, Arch, Cantilever, etc...) and solve for the internal forces acting upon the bridge. With these values I determined the total load my bridge design could handle. Finally I calculated the total "figure of merit" to see how many kN/m my bridge could handle. Withstanding Weather

Final Project for my Cornerstone of Engineering Class. The theme was resiliency in engineering; our group went with the strength of building materials and their resilience to weather around the world. We portrayed this with an interactive game using different building blocks which could be used to make structures. These structures were then judged by our algorithm and determined to be resilient or not in different settings around the world. The full report can be found below.
